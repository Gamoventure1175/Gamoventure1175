studying, and improving.

The first update has not been published yet. It will be written once the current
focus is supplied, rather than inferred from general interests or past experience.

## Update format

Each revision should answer three questions:

1. What am I building or studying?
2. What engineering question am I trying to understand?
3. What changed since the previous update?

The page should stay concise and carry an explicit last-updated date.
