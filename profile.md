# Profile

I am a junior software developer with experience working on backend services, web
applications, and data-driven systems. I am especially interested in backend
development, databases, and understanding how software works under the hood.

I am still learning. I try to write clear code, ask useful questions, and improve by
building things rather than presenting every interest as expertise.

## Beyond software

Away from software, I spend a lot of time speedcubing. My personal best is 8.76
seconds and my typical solves are 10–15 seconds. I enjoy the rhythm of spotting a
pattern, trying a move, and going again. I also type consistently above 90 words per
minute.

Portal is my favourite game and one I happily replay. I love its puzzles, atmosphere,
and the moment a solution suddenly clicks; the wider Half-Life universe carries that
same sense of discovery.

These are simply things I enjoy returning to—sometimes to improve, sometimes just
because they are fun.
