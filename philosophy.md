# Notes I am learning by

## Keep it clear

Readable code is easier to revisit, explain, and improve. I try to choose the clearest
solution I understand instead of making something look more advanced than it is.

## Learn the fundamentals

I enjoy asking why a technology works, not only how to use it. That curiosity guides
what I study, while building projects shows me where my understanding is incomplete.

## Write down useful context

Short notes about decisions and unexpected behavior save time later. I am learning to
treat documentation as part of the work rather than an extra step.

## Keep improving

These are preferences I am practicing, not claims that I have mastered them. They will
change as I gain experience.
